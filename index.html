how it works

  • Add toDo items at the top of the page, and choose their level of priority.
  • When hovering your mouse, either move items into the completed box (green check) or delete them (red cross).
  • Sort your toDo items (assending priority or desending priority) by choosing one of the 2 circlular buttons on the left hand side
  • Clear your completed items by selecting the clear button
